ELL3932 단편영화의예술(Art of the Short Fiction Film) 
The Art of the Short Fiction Film begins in a basement in Paris in 1895, with the Lumiere brothers' screening of 
twelve short films, an evening program that heralded the advent of the modern industry of the moving picture. it 
concludes with the contemporary dominance of YouTube, Vimeo and Netflix, in an age in which digital 
filmmaking has catapulted the short film once again to international prominence. Between these two dates, the 
class will look at the subordinate role of the one-and two-reeler "short subjects" during the Hollywood Studio 
Era; analyze the role of the European and North American film school and international film festivals in the 
elevation of the short film after the mid-1950s; study classical and post-classical cinematic technique; trace the 
reimagining of the Hollywood film studios in the digital era by examining the role of the short film, both as filmed 
scenario and as possible harbinger of a change or mutation in the viewing habits of its global audience. 
ELL3934 아포칼립스소설(Apocalypse Novels) (2022년 개설) 
Speculative fiction, of which apocalyptic fiction is a sub-genre, has become an increasing focus of attention in 
literary studies. The texts studied in the course will enable the class to examine such contemporary dilemmas as 
social disintegration, civil strife, economic breakdown, and environmental catastrophe. How do the warnings 
given by representations of the apocalypse help us to understand the causes of disaster and collapse, and how 
might we be able to avoid them?

ELL4302 고급영문법(Advanced English Grammar) 
The course will concentrate on aspects of English grammar that have been traditionally described, as well 
as cover different approaches to grammar teaching and learning, helping students develop a greater 
understanding of how these areas of English grammar work. We will closely examine common 
grammatical problems that arise in producing and understanding English sentences and consider various 
ways of addressing the problems.
